Published Papers

Resource Planning System for Organisations: A Soft Computing Approach

International Journal of Services, Economics and Management, 2021  ·  Vol. 12(3), pp. 272–293
with Sina Gholami, Reza Sheikh, and Shib Sankar Sana
A soft computing framework for organisational resource planning under uncertainty — fuzzy logic and heuristics as a tractable alternative to rigid optimisation.
Develops a soft computing framework for organisational resource planning, combining fuzzy logic and heuristic methods to model uncertainty in complex service systems. The approach offers a tractable alternative to rigid optimisation models in environments with incomplete information. The method is validated on a real organisational planning problem and shown to outperform classical approaches on key performance metrics.

The Analysis and Interpretation of Quality Cost Using Rough Set Theory: A Case Study from the Ceramic Industry

International Journal of Service and Computing Oriented Manufacturing, 2021  ·  Vol. 4(1), pp. 11–31
with Samira Rezaei Toroghi, Reza Sheikh, and Shib Sankar Sana
Applying rough set theory to quality cost structures in manufacturing — extracting interpretable decision rules under data imprecision.
Applies rough set theory to analyse and interpret quality cost structures in manufacturing. Using data from the ceramic industry, the paper identifies cost drivers and decision rules that support quality management under conditions of uncertainty and imprecise measurement. The rough set approach provides an interpretable, data-driven alternative to classical quality cost analysis methods.
